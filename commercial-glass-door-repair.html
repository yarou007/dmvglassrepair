Commercial Glass Door Repair

If a storefront or entry door is sagging, scraping, sticking, or not latching, it’s a safety and security issue. We repair commercial aluminum glass doors and their hardware so your entrance operates correctly.

What We Repair

Commercial glass doors are a system: the door leaf, frame, pivots, closers, locks, and the glass itself. We diagnose the issue and repair the components causing the problem.

  • Door alignment and adjustment (binding, dragging, hinge-side issues)
  • Door closers (speed control, leaking, replacement)
  • Pivots, hinges, and pivots/arms (wear, loose mounting, damaged parts)
  • Locks, latches, and panic hardware (function and alignment)
  • Glass replacement in doors, sidelites, and transoms

Signs Your Door Needs Service

Door Won’t Close

Usually closer issues, pivot wear, or frame alignment problems.

Door Scrapes the Floor

Common on busy entrances—often a pivot or hinge adjustment is needed.

Latch Doesn’t Catch

Strike alignment, lockset wear, or door position can cause security issues.

Broken Door Glass

We can secure the opening and replace the glass panel for a clean finish.
