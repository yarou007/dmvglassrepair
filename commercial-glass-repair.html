Commercial Glass Services

Commercial work is about safety, traffic flow, and minimizing downtime. We support retail storefronts, offices, restaurants, and managed properties with a clear scope and clean install.

  • Storefront glass repair and replacement (tempered / laminated)
  • Damaged sidelites and transoms
  • Interior commercial glass and tenant improvement glazing
  • Coordination with door hardware service when needed

Safety & Code Compliance

We confirm safety glazing requirements for door lites, sidelites, and high-traffic openings. Our team follows ANSI Z97.1 and CPSC 16 CFR 1201 guidance and can advise on UL-rated glass when added protection is required.

See Safety Compliance and Tempered Glass Replacement for more details.

Energy-Efficient Glass Upgrades

If you want better insulation or lower HVAC costs, we can quote double-pane and Low-E glass options that meet energy-efficient glazing requirements.

Learn more on our Energy-Efficient Glass page.

Emergency Board-Up & Next-Step Repairs

If an opening is unsecured, we prioritize safety and security first. After emergency board-up, we coordinate replacement glass and schedule installation to restore operations.
