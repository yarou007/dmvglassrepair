How to Secure a Business After a Break-In

After a break-in, the right first steps protect people, preserve evidence, and reduce revenue loss. This checklist helps owners and managers act quickly and safely.

Step 1: Protect People and Preserve Evidence

Keep staff and customers away from the damaged zone, contact authorities, and document the scene before cleanup. Once approved, secure the opening with emergency board-up.

Need immediate secure-up? Call 703-244-0559 for 24/7 emergency board-up service.

Step 2: Secure the Building Envelope

Replace or reinforce vulnerable openings, check lock function, and verify door closer performance. If replacement glass is not immediately available, maintain secure temporary protection.

  • Board-up exposed openings and isolate broken glass zones
  • Check lock cylinders, strike plates, and panic hardware function
  • Coordinate replacement timeline with staff and landlord teams

Step 3: Plan Reopening and Permanent Repairs

Share photos, incident timing, and property access notes so your glass and door team can prioritize permanent restoration with minimal disruption.
