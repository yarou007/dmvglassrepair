Why Tempered Glass Is Essential for Safety

Tempered glass is designed to reduce injury risk and meet safety glazing standards for storefronts, doors, and shower enclosures.

Where Tempered Glass Is Required

  • Door lites, sidelites, and storefront openings
  • Shower doors and enclosures
  • High-traffic glass areas in commercial spaces

Safety Standards That Apply

Two common safety glazing standards are ANSI Z97.1 and CPSC 16 CFR 1201. These standards guide impact testing and safety performance.

Read more on our Safety Compliance page.

Signs You Need Replacement

  • Cracked or chipped tempered panels
  • Loose door lites or framing movement
  • Damage near hinges, pivots, or hardware
