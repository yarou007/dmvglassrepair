Signs Your Door Closer Needs Repair

A failing door closer can create daily safety problems, customer frustration, and lock-up issues. Catching early signs helps avoid expensive emergency calls later.

Most Common Warning Signs

Watch for oil leakage, slamming doors, incomplete closing, or latching failure. These symptoms often mean the closer needs adjustment, seal repair, or replacement.

Door not closing safely? Call 703-244-0559 for fast commercial door closer repair.

When to Call a Pro Immediately

Call quickly when doors do not latch, slam hard, or drift open in windy conditions. Entrances that fail to secure can create security and life-safety risk.

  • Oil around the closer body or arm connection points
  • Door slams, drags, or fails to close fully
  • Latch misses, lock alignment issues, or uneven swing speed

How to Speed Up Door Closer Repairs

Send photos of the closer body, arm, hinge side, and strike area. Include when the issue occurs most often (peak traffic, weather, or after-hours lockup).
