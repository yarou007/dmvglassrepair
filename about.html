About Capital Glass Door

Capital Glass Door provides glass repair, door repair, and locksmith services for residential and commercial customers. We focus on clear communication, jobsite safety, and minimizing downtime.

Our Focus

Commercial properties have unique requirements—tenant access, business hours, security needs, and higher-traffic door systems. Our workflow is designed for offices, retail storefronts, restaurants, multi-tenant buildings, warehouses, and managed properties.

We help both commercial and residential customers. If you need emergency board-up, storefront glass, door repair, locksmith service, or shower door work, we’ll guide you to the right solution.

How We Work

The fastest projects are the ones with the fewest surprises. When you reach out, we’ll ask for a couple of practical details so we can price and schedule accurately:

  • Location and access details (loading dock, after-hours access, tenant contacts)
  • Photos of the damaged area (especially for emergency calls)
  • Glass type (if known) and approximate size
  • Door hardware symptoms (closer leaking, latch not engaging, panic bar sticking)

For emergencies, we can help secure the opening first and then coordinate replacement glass to restore your storefront quickly.
