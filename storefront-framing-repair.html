Storefront Framing Repair

Storefront glass problems aren’t always “just the glass.” We repair commercial aluminum storefront framing so panels fit correctly, entrances operate smoothly, and water intrusion is reduced.

What Storefront Framing Repair Covers

A storefront system includes aluminum framing, mullions, gaskets, sealants, and glass stops that hold the glass in place. If the frame is loose, out of alignment, or leaking, it can damage glass and hardware over time.

  • Loose or damaged mullions and framing components
  • Glazing support issues (movement, rattling, uneven setting)
  • Sealant and perimeter leak repairs
  • Entrance alignment support (in coordination with door hardware service)

Common Problems We Fix

Water Leaks

Failed sealants, missing gaskets, or perimeter gaps can cause interior water damage.

Loose Glass

Movement in the frame can lead to stress cracks and unsafe conditions.

Entrance Misalignment

Storefront framing issues can contribute to doors sticking or not latching correctly.

Impact Damage

After a hit, framing components may need repair before new glass is installed.
