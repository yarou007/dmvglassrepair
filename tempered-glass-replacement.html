Tempered Glass Replacement

Broken storefront or door glass? We replace commercial tempered glass panels for storefronts, entry doors, sidelites, and transoms. Emergency board-up is available when the opening needs to be secured right away.

Where Tempered Glass Is Used

Tempered glass is commonly used in commercial entrances because it’s designed to break into small pieces instead of sharp shards. Typical locations include storefront systems, aluminum-framed doors, and large glass panels near walkways.

  • Storefront glass panels and windows
  • Glass entry doors (door lites) and sidelites
  • Transoms above doors
  • Interior commercial glazing where required

Common Reasons for Replacement

We see these issues most often on commercial properties:

  • Impact breaks (accidents, deliveries, vandalism)
  • Cracked or shattered door glass panels
  • Loose glazing / failed setting blocks causing movement
  • Water intrusion and sealant failure around the perimeter

If your opening is unsecured, call 703-244-0559 for emergency response or review Board-Up (Emergency).

Our Process (Commercial)

1) Identify the Glass

We confirm tempered vs. laminated, thickness, tint, and whether it’s part of a storefront system.

2) Measure & Quote

We measure the opening, check the frame, and provide a clear scope for replacement.

3) Secure (If Needed)

We board-up and secure the opening to reduce risk and keep the site safe.

4) Replace & Clean Up

We install the new panel, verify fit and seal, then clean the area for a professional finish.
