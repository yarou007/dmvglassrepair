Door Problems We Fix

Our door repair service covers the most common commercial and residential door issues across Washington DC and the DMV:

Door Won't Latch

Alignment issues, strike adjustment, and hardware wear that prevents a secure close — resolved quickly and professionally.

Door Closer Issues

Slamming doors, leaking closers, or slow-closing mechanisms. We adjust, repair, or replace closers to restore proper function.

Locks & Hardware

Sticking locks, worn handles, broken deadbolts, and hardware failures that stop normal door operation.

Panic Hardware

Panic bar repairs, exit device adjustments, and ADA-compliant hardware installation for commercial buildings in DC.

Commercial Door Repair

We service all types of commercial doors including aluminum glass doors, storefront entry systems, and heavy-duty commercial exit doors. If the issue includes broken door glass, see Commercial Glass Door Repair.

Residential Door Repair

We also provide door repair for homeowners across Washington DC, Arlington, Alexandria, and the DMV. Whether it's a sticking front door, a broken patio door, or a garage entry door that won't latch, we can help.
