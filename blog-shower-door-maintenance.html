How to Maintain Your Shower Doors

Simple maintenance steps keep shower doors smooth, quiet, and leak-free.

Weekly Care

  • Wipe glass with a soft cloth or squeegee after use
  • Clean tracks and hinges to avoid buildup
  • Use non-abrasive cleaners to protect hardware finish

Monthly Checkups

  • Inspect hinges and rollers for play or looseness
  • Check seals and sweeps for wear
  • Confirm doors close cleanly without rubbing

When to Call a Pro

  • Door is sagging or scraping the threshold
  • Glass shows chips or cracks near hardware
  • Water leaks persist despite new seals
