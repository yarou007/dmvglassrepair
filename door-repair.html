Door Repair (Residential & Commercial)

When a door won’t close, won’t latch, or won’t secure, it becomes a safety and security problem. We repair door systems and hardware across DC, VA, and MD (including Baltimore). Call 703-244-0559.

Problems We Fix

Door Won’t Latch

Alignment issues, strike adjustment, and hardware wear that prevents a secure close.

Door Closer Issues

Slamming, leaking, or slow closing. We adjust or replace closers when needed.

Locks & Hardware

Sticking locks, worn handles, and hardware failures that stop normal operation.

Storefront & Commercial Door Support

We service commercial aluminum glass doors, entry systems, and related door hardware. If the issue includes broken door glass, see Commercial Glass Door Repair.
