Commercial Door Hardware Repair

A misaligned door, leaking closer, or failing lock can stop business operations and create safety issues. We repair and replace commercial door hardware for storefronts and building entrances.

Hardware We Commonly Service

Closers & Adjustments

Door closer replacement, tuning, and related adjustments to restore smooth operation.

Locks & Latches

Commercial lock hardware diagnosis and replacement (scope varies by door system).

Hinges, Pivots, & Alignment

Hardware wear and alignment issues that cause sticking, slamming, or gaps.

When Door Hardware Becomes an Emergency

If a door won’t secure, won’t close, or creates a safety risk, call. If glass is broken too, see Emergency Glass Repair.
