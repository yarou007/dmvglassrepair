Emergency Board-Up for Commercial Storefront Glass

Broken storefront glass is an emergency security issue. We provide emergency board-up for businesses to secure openings, reduce risk, and start the commercial glass repair service process.

Call 703-244-0559

What We Do (Emergency Service)

Our emergency workflow is built for commercial sites: retail storefronts, offices, and managed properties. We focus on safety, security, and a clear plan for replacement.

  • Secure broken glass openings with board-up when needed
  • Reduce risk to staff, customers, and tenants
  • Capture details to plan storefront glass repair or replacement
  • Coordinate next-step repairs for commercial glass systems

What Helps Us Respond Faster

  • Exact address and best on-site contact
  • Photos of the damage (inside/outside)
  • Whether the opening is street-facing and needs immediate security
  • Any access restrictions (security desk, loading dock, after-hours rules)

After the Board-Up

Once the site is stabilized, we coordinate the right glass type and schedule installation. For storefront systems, we may also coordinate related door hardware service if it affects operation.

For more background and photos, see the existing Board-Up (Emergency) page. For storefront replacement details, see Storefront Glass.
