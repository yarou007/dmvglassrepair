Frequently Asked Questions

What causes foggy double-pane windows?

Foggy double-pane windows usually result from failed seals that allow moisture between panes, reducing visibility and insulation.

Do you replace IGUs without replacing the full frame?

In many cases, yes. We often replace only the insulated glass unit if the frame is still serviceable.
