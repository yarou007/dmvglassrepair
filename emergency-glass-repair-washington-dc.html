How Our Emergency Glass Repair Works

Our emergency glass repair process is designed for speed and safety. We understand that a broken window or door is a security risk that requires immediate attention.

1) Immediate Intake

Call 703-244-0559 — we'll ask for the address, access status, and photos if available to assess the emergency.

2) Fast Dispatch

Our emergency teams are positioned across Washington DC and the DMV for rapid deployment. We prioritize unsecured openings.

3) Site Stabilization

We secure the opening with emergency board-up to protect your property, employees, and customers while replacement glass is sourced.

4) Glass Replacement

We coordinate replacement glass based on the storefront system, glass type, and safety requirements — then complete a professional install.
