How Long Does Storefront Glass Replacement Take?

Most business owners ask one question first: how quickly can we get the storefront secure and fully replaced? Timelines vary based on glass type, size, and site access.

Typical Timeline by Project Type

Emergency secure-up can often happen the same day. Standard replacements may be faster when measurements are straightforward, while custom panels and specialty glass can take longer.

Need immediate secure-up? Call 703-244-0559 for 24/7 emergency board-up service.

What Affects Replacement Speed

Lead time changes based on tempered vs laminated requirements, glass thickness, panel size, and whether hardware or framing repairs are needed before final install.

  • Glass type and thickness availability
  • Custom panel sizing and fabrication queues
  • Building access windows, permits, and tenant operating hours

How to Speed Up the Job

Send clear photos, approximate measurements, location details, and the best contact onsite. These details reduce back-and-forth and help confirm material requirements sooner.
