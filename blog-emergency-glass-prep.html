Emergency Glass Prep Checklist

When glass breaks, safety and security come first. Use this checklist to protect people, secure the opening, and speed up response.

Step 1: Protect People

  • Keep staff or family away from the damaged area
  • Block off the opening if it is in a high-traffic space
  • Wear gloves when cleaning loose shards

Step 2: Secure the Opening

  • Use a temporary barrier if you can safely do so
  • Take photos of the damage from inside and outside
  • Note whether the opening is street-facing or exposed

Step 3: Call for Emergency Board-Up

Call 703-244-0559 with the address, access notes, and photos. We can board-up the opening and schedule the replacement.

Related services: Emergency Board-Up, Board-Up (Emergency).

Step 4: Plan the Replacement

We confirm the right glass type (tempered, laminated, or insulated) and coordinate installation with minimal downtime.
