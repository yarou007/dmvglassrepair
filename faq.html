Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for business owners and property managers looking for commercial glass repair, storefront glass replacement, door hardware service, and emergency board-up.

Emergency & Response

Do you offer 24/7 emergency commercial glass repair?

Yes—emergency response is available 24/7. Call 703-244-0559. If it’s safe, send photos and your address so we can advise next steps.

What happens first when glass is broken?

Safety and security come first. Depending on the opening and risk, we may secure the area (board-up / temporary protection) and then schedule replacement glass installation.

How quickly can you arrive?

Response time depends on location, access, and current workload. Call with your address and urgency and we’ll give the most accurate ETA we can.

Services & Scope

Who do you work with?

We work with businesses, property managers, and homeowners across the DMV for glass, doors, and locksmith needs.

Can you repair commercial door hardware like closers and panic bars?

Yes. We service common hardware issues including door closers, pivots, hinges, and panic hardware. If you can share a photo of the hardware and describe the symptom, quoting is faster.

What types of glass do you work with?

Commercial jobs often involve tempered, laminated, and insulated glass. The correct type depends on the opening and local code requirements. We’ll confirm what’s appropriate during quoting.

Quotes & Scheduling

How do you quote commercial glass work?

We typically request photos, address, basic measurements, and access constraints. Some projects require an onsite assessment depending on complexity.

Can you work around business hours?

Often yes, depending on your access requirements and the type of install. Tell us your constraints and we’ll propose a realistic schedule.

How do I request a quote?

Call 703-244-0559. For emergency situations, calling is fastest. For scheduled work, texts/photos are welcome.
